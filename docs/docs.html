About me: Hi! My name is Tara and I am 21 years old. I am going to Penn State Behrend to pursue a degree in Digital Media with a minor in English. I am interested in data visualization and web design. I enjoy playing videogames, reading, and anything art related. About this project: This all started with a simple playthrough of The Sims 4. I wanted to decorate a house to look straight out of the 1970s. To do this, I first tried to find references like magazines and real photos. My findings were slim to none. Many interior magazine archives are either pay-walled or have blank pages. This was not enough for me. I decided that I want to get magazines, take photos of them, and mark them up to the best of my ability. I want to give people access to this information. The 1970's was an era that was somewhat forgotten. The boom of the internet took over many peoples lives, so unfortunately interior design magazines got left behind when information started to become digitized. My main goal for this project is to document interior design in the 1970s as authentically as I can. I want to make the information readily available for interior designers, students, and for fun decorating purposes.
